Fill the land, not landfills—

As a student, be on the lookout for any composting bins around your area, and try to use them whenever you have the opportunity to. Guides are scattered around each dining area to help identify which items are compostable, and which aren't. Together, we can become a greener, cleaner campus. Don’t waste your waste!
