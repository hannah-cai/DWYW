Fill the land, not landfills—

As a student, be on the lookout for any composting bins around your area, and try to use them whenever you have the opportunity to. Go to a dining area that offers composting to pick up an id-card-sized composting guide, located by one of these posters:

The guides will lead you from the poster to the nearest composting bin. Keep it in your wallet or id-holder to refer back to! It’s a reminder to keep composting, and a manual on how to make the process as simple as possible.

While CMU works on expanding its composting facilities across campus, try to eat at dining locations that offer compost bins, or use compostable products such as Resnik’s utensils, which decompose naturally. Use this website as a resource to help you become an informed and involved composter, and get other people in on the cause too. Tell your friends! One of the biggest steps towards changing composting culture on campus is to raise awareness on how easy and rewarding it is. Together, we can become a cleaner, greener campus. Don’t waste your waste!
