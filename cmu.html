Getting down and dirty—

Carnegie Mellon aims to raise awareness and environmentally-friendly behavior within the campus community. To this end, CMU Dining Service and AgRecycle are actively working with the school’s food vendors to place composting bins around eating areas. Currently, there are composting bins at Schatz, The Underground, Resnik, and the Tepper Quad. From workers—who are encouraged to compost waste as they cook—to the student body, everyone can pitch in. CMU is also in the process of switching from plastic products to compostable products, so that plates, utensils, and cups will be able to be responsibly thrown away.
